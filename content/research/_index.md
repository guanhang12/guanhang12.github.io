My research centers on advancing sustainable building energy systems through modeling, optimization, and intelligent control. Below are my three main research areas.

---

## Energy-Efficient Drying Systems

Leading a DOE-funded project on decarbonizing industrial food drying using desiccant-wheel heat pump (DWHP) systems. This work involves desiccant wheel modeling and CFD-based geometric optimization, system virtual testbed development, life cycle analysis, and future hardware-in-loop deployment.

**Project:** [Desiccant-Wheel Heat Pump System](/research/desiccant-wheel-heat-pump/)
**Publications:** [ASHRAE 2024](https://scholar.google.com/citations?view_op=view_citation&hl=en&user=A6TRzYYAAAAJ&citation_for_view=A6TRzYYAAAAJ:qjMakFHDy7sC) · [ASHRAE 2025](https://store.accuristech.com/standards/or-25-c067-optimizing-the-geometric-design-of-desiccant-wheels-for-drying-controllability-and-desiccant-use?product_id=3017352)

## Geothermal Energy Systems

Developing scalable borefield models using scientific machine learning for real-time advanced control of ground-source heat pump (GSHP) systems. Also conducted borefield sizing and water-loop GSHP system evaluation for multi-family buildings during a Ph.D. internship at Pacific Northwest National Laboratory.

**Projects:** [Borefield Modeling & Control](/research/borefield-modeling/) · [GSHP at PNNL](/research/pnnl-gshp/)

---

## Smart Building Technologies

Contributing to intelligent building systems including automated HVAC design tools (pipe routing via multi-agent ant colony optimization), infrared-based fault detection for hot water pipes, and multi-zone infection risk assessment for airborne virus transmission on cruise ships.

**Projects:** [BEMBOTS](/research/bembots-hvac-design/) · [Fault Diagnosis](/research/fault-diagnosis-ir/) · [Infection Risk](/research/infection-risk-assessment/)
**Publications:** [B&E 2022](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.buildenv.2022.109111) · [AiC 2025](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.autcon.2025.106359) · [AiC 2022](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.autcon.2022.104264) · [Buildings 2023](https://doi.org/10.3390/buildings13092350)
