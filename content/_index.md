## About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Mechanical Engineering at Texas A&M University, advised by Dr. Zheng O'Neill. My research focuses on developing energy-efficient HVAC systems through advanced modeling, scientific machine learning, and optimization. Prior to TAMU, I received my M.Eng. from Tongji University and B.Eng. from Zhengzhou University, both in Building Environment and Energy Engineering.

## Research Interests

- Energy-efficient HVAC systems and desiccant-wheel heat pump technologies
- Geothermal borefield modeling and control using scientific machine learning
- Building automation, intelligent fault detection, and HVAC system design
- Indoor environmental quality and airborne disease transmission modeling
